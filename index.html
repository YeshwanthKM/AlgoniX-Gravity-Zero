GRAVITY ZERO

Manipulate gravity to guide your avatar to the exit.

HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Gravity Zero!

  • Setup Phase: Place your gravity zones while the character is stationary.
  • Your goal is to guide them to the green exit portal while avoiding the red hazards.
  • Select a Gravity Direction (⬇️, ⬆️, ⬅️, ➡️) and click on the grid to deploy a zone.
  • Once ready, press LAUNCH SHIFT to start the movement!
  • The character moves based on the latest gravity zone they passed through.
